About Us

ACM brings together computing educators, researchers, and professionals to inspire dialogue, share resources, and address the field's challenges. As the world’s largest computing society, ACM strengthens the profession's collective voice through strong leadership, promotion of the highest standards, and recognition of technical excellence. ACM supports the professional growth of its members by providing opportunities for life‐long learning, career development, and professional networking.

Founded at the dawn of the computer age, ACM’s reach extends to every part of the globe, with more than half of its more than 100,000 members residing outside the U.S. Its growing membership has led to Councils in Europe, India, and China, fostering networking opportunities that strengthen ties within and across countries and technical communities. Their actions enhance ACM’s ability to raise awareness of computing’s important technical, educational, and social issues around the world. 

  Mission

    The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) stands as a preeminent global organization, uniting professionals and enthusiasts alike, with a steadfast dedication to advancing the multifaceted realms of computing. At its core, ACM is a hub for the exchange of knowledge, fostering an environment where the intricate interplay of art, science, engineering, and practical application in computing can thrive. With a mission that transcends borders, ACM serves the interests of both professionals and the broader public.
    ACM's commitment to openness and information exchange is exemplified through its multifaceted approach. The organization not only organizes and sponsors an array of conferences but also publishes a rich tapestry of journals and proceedings, disseminating cutting-edge research and discoveries to a global audience. This commitment extends beyond the professional realm, as ACM actively advocates for the highest standards of professionalism and ethics, addressing the societal implications of computing.
    Furthermore, ACM's influence is palpable in its support for educational initiatives. Through the creation of educational resources, curriculum recommendations, and online courses, ACM empowers the next generation of computing enthusiasts and professionals. This commitment is reinforced through student chapters at universities worldwide, providing a platform for students to engage, learn, and contribute to the dynamic landscape of computing.
    In navigating the intricate landscape of computing, ACM's Special Interest Groups (SIGs) serve as dedicated communities within the larger framework, addressing specific subfields or thematic areas. These SIGs organize focused activities, events, and publications, allowing members to delve deeper into their areas of interest while contributing to the collective knowledge pool.
    In essence, ACM is not merely an organization; it is a dynamic ecosystem that propels the diverse facets of computing forward. By embodying principles of openness, professionalism, education, and community, ACM stands as a beacon, guiding the global computing community towards innovation, excellence, and responsible technological advancement.

  Vision

    ACM will persist as the foremost global computing society, maintaining its status as the preeminent hub for professionals, researchers, and enthusiasts in the field. Positioned at the forefront of technological evolution, ACM will continue to lead, providing a dynamic platform for the exchange of ideas, knowledge, and advancements in computing. With an unwavering commitment to excellence, ACM's influence will endure, shaping the trajectory of the computing landscape, fostering collaboration, and upholding the highest standards of professionalism. As a beacon of innovation, ACM is poised to navigate the ever-changing realm of computing, ensuring its continued prominence on the global stage.

Hacked22


In the vibrant landscape of the #Hacked22 hackathon, we meticulously crafted an environment that pulsated with innovation, collaboration, and creative ideation, responding dynamically to the ever-evolving industry. The challenge presented to participants was no ordinary task; it was a complex problem statement that set the stage for 24 hours of intense coding and logical exploration, unfolding seamlessly at our campus during the monumental 67th annual milestone fest.

Our primary objective was to beckon aspiring engineers, developers, and mobile and web hackers, cultivating a space that not only stimulated creativity but also wholeheartedly embraced innovation. The hackathon's distinctive open theme empowered participants to select their unique problem statements, fostering a rich tapestry of diversity in project exploration.

Elevating the experience further, we broke away from the confines of the digital realm, taking the entire event offline. This strategic shift not only expanded our reach but also allowed us to engage with a broader audience, creating an unforgettable experience for everyone involved. The generous support from sponsors, including companies like Herody, played a pivotal role in amplifying the success of our event, enabling us to enhance the overall experience for participants.

Our promotional efforts were seamlessly executed through platforms such as Unstop, GitHub, and Discord, creating a cohesive and interactive virtual space. The #Hacked22 hackathon transcended the mere realm of a coding challenge; it emerged as a vibrant celebration of innovation, community, and the boundless possibilities inherent in the ever-evolving landscape of technology. As participants delved into their projects, the hackathon became a testament to the limitless potential of collaboration and creative exploration within the technological sphere.

Hacktober Fest

Hacktoberfest, orchestrated by DigitalOcean, unfolds as a month-long revelry dedicated to open source software enthusiasts. Embracing the collaborative spirit of the open-source community, this annual event spans the entirety of October, providing a golden opportunity for students to immerse themselves in the world of open source. The primary focus is on guiding students through their journey of contributing to diverse open-source projects. By actively participating and registering early, participants not only lend their skills to valuable projects but also stand a chance to accrue enticing rewards.

The allure of Hacktoberfest lies not just in the thrill of coding and collaboration but also in the tangible rewards awaiting contributors. Exclusive swag, featuring coveted stickers and personalized T-shirts, serves as a tangible acknowledgment of their commitment to the open-source ethos. This initiative not only cultivates a sense of community among participants but also acts as a gateway for students to establish themselves within the expansive and collaborative landscape of open source. It's a celebration that transcends coding challenges, encouraging a shared commitment to the principles of open collaboration and innovation throughout the month of October.

Smart India Hackathon

The Smart India Hackathon (SIH) stands as an annual nationwide endeavor in India, urging and challenging students to craft innovative solutions for authentic problems encountered by government organizations and industries. As one of the world's largest hackathons, it is orchestrated by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, and is distinguished for its emphasis on problem-solving, student participation, innovation, competition, and the pragmatic resolution of real-world issues.

With a core focus on fostering problem-solving skills, encouraging student engagement, promoting innovation, and tackling genuine challenges, the Smart India Hackathon serves as a platform to channel the creativity and technical acumen of India's youth. The overarching objective is to discover practical solutions that contribute significantly to the nation's development. This event stands out as a collaborative effort that unites students, government agencies, and industries, fostering collective problem-solving through the lens of technology and innovation. It symbolizes a convergence of ideas, expertise, and enthusiasm aimed at addressing critical challenges and steering India towards a future defined by technological ingenuity and sustainable development.

Sustainability Hackathon Challenge

We orchestrated the Sustainability Hackathon Challenge (SHC), a nationwide hackathon with the overarching goal of enlightening young minds about sustainable development. Serving as a regional host, our primary mission encompassed ensuring robust participation and conducting bootcamps to guide students in formulating sustainable solutions to global challenges. The hackathon bootcamp served as a remarkable platform for students to showcase their inventive ideas and enhance their technical skills. With sustainability as the central theme, participants delved into projects addressing critical issues related to the environment, economic development, and more.

The event commenced with an enlightening introduction by Professor Davinder Singh, shedding light on the significance of sustainability and the impact of technology on the environment. Dr. Arunaditya Sahay, a seasoned expert in sustainability, followed with a talk, sharing his experiences and knowledge, thus inspiring participants to think innovatively. Dr. Sahay's insightful discourse emphasized the intersection of technology and sustainability.

Throughout the hackathon, participants collaborated in teams, working diligently to devise inventive solutions for pressing sustainability issues. Projects ranged from eco-friendly transportation systems to sustainable waste management solutions. To foster a dynamic and energized atmosphere, refreshments were provided to all participants. The well-organized event ensured that participants had unfettered access to the resources necessary for the development of their solutions, culminating in a successful showcase of creativity, collaboration, and commitment to addressing crucial sustainability challenges.

Why to Join ACM?

    1.Professional Networking: ACM provides a vast network of professionals, researchers, and enthusiasts in the computing field. Joining ACM connects you with like-minded individuals, offering opportunities for collaboration, mentorship, and knowledge exchange.
    2. Access to Cutting-Edge Research: As an ACM member, you gain access to a wealth of research through ACM's extensive digital library. Stay abreast of the latest developments in computer science, artificial intelligence, and other emerging fields.
    3. Career Development: ACM offers resources for career development, including job boards, career services, and opportunities to showcase your skills. Stay informed about job openings, connect with potential employers, and advance your career in the computing industry.
    4. Participation in Conferences and Events: ACM organizes and sponsors conferences worldwide. By joining, you can attend these events, present your research, and immerse yourself in the latest trends and innovations within the computing community.
    5. Educational Resources: ACM provides valuable educational resources, including online courses, webinars, and curriculum recommendations. Whether you are a student or a professional, these resources can enhance your knowledge and skills.
    6. Community Engagement: ACM fosters a sense of community among its members. Engage in local chapters, online forums, and special interest groups (SIGs) to connect with peers who share your interests and passions.

    In essence, ACM is not just an organization; it is a dynamic community that empowers you to overcome mental barriers, unleash your potential, and thrive in both your technical pursuits and the broader social aspects of your journey. Join ACM to become a part of a global movement that ignites innovation and success at every turn.
